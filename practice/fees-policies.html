Insurance

I provide therapy in two distinct contexts:

Private pay through my independent practice, Existenz Psychotherapy

Insurance-based therapy through a separate group practice

Insurance billing, plan eligibility, and scheduling are managed exclusively through the group practice. Private-pay services are not billed to insurance.

Details about how to schedule under each option are provided on the Contact page.

Good Faith Estimate

If you are paying privately and not using insurance, you have the right to receive a Good Faith Estimate of the expected cost of services, in accordance with the No Surprises Act.

A written estimate is provided upon request or when scheduling.

If the final charges exceed the estimate by $400 or more, you have the right to dispute the bill.

More information:

www.cms.gov/nosurprises/consumers

FederalPPDRQuestions@cms.hhs.gov

1-800-985-3059

Superbills

I generally do not provide superbills.

In limited circumstances, superbill arrangements may be discussed prior to the start of treatment. Superbills require a diagnostic impression, which is always addressed transparently and collaboratively.

Cancellations

Please provide at least 48 hours’ notice for cancellations or rescheduling.

Less than 48 hours’ notice: $100 for the first occurrence

Repeated late cancellations or missed sessions: full session fee

If I am able to fill the time slot, the cancellation fee will be waived.
