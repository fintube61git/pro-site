Clinical psychology and self-directed tools

I provide IFS-informed psychotherapy for complex trauma and build free, self-directed web tools for exploring internal parts and systems. My work also includes peer-reviewed research, professional education, and experience in regulatory and quality systems within highly regulated healthcare environments.

Across this work, the aim is to externalize internal experience: supporting contact with what is present in the moment and understanding how those experiences function within a larger internal system.

Clinical psychology
