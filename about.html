About

I’m a licensed psychologist providing trauma‑informed, evidence‑based psychotherapy. My approach integrates IFS, humanistic–existential perspectives, somatic awareness, mindfulness‑based therapies, and ACT. I aim for practical change without losing depth or nuance.

Before clinical practice, I worked 15+ years across biotech, pharma, and medical devices in regulatory strategy, software quality, data integrity, and information security. That background helps me translate complexity into clear, grounded care.
