About

I am a licensed psychologist providing trauma‑informed, evidence‑based psychotherapy. My clinical work integrates IFS, humanistic–existential approaches, mindfulness‑based therapies, somatic methods, and ACT.

Before becoming a clinician, I spent over 15 years in the biotechnology, pharmaceutical, and medical device industries (regulatory strategy, data integrity auditing, software quality, information security). This blend of clinical and technical expertise informs my work as a therapist, educator, and consultant.
