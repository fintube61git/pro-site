Services

I provide trauma‑informed, evidence‑based psychotherapy. My work integrates Internal Family Systems (IFS), Trauma‑Informed Stabilization Treatment (TIST), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), humanistic–existential therapy, somatic trauma therapy, mindfulness‑based approaches, and motivational interviewing. We’ll stabilize first, then go deeper as it’s safe and useful.

Who I work with

  • Adults navigating anxiety, depression, trauma, identity, or meaning.
  • Clinicians, founders, and professionals under sustained pressure.
  • Clients seeking depth‑aware but practical psychotherapy.

How we’ll work

  • IFS / parts work: meeting protective parts; strengthening Self‑leadership.
  • Stabilization & somatic: building safety, regulation, and body awareness.
  • ACT: clarifying values; taking workable steps toward what matters.
  • Mindfulness & MI: present‑moment skills and collaborative change.
