Clinical Services

I provide trauma-informed, evidence-based psychotherapy integrating Internal Family Systems (IFS), Trauma-Informed Stabilization Treatment (TIST), Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT), humanistic–existential approaches, somatic trauma therapy, mindfulness-based therapies, and motivational interviewing.

Individuals

Anxiety, depression, trauma stabilization, identity and meaning work, grief, career stress.

Groups

Skills and process groups focused on regulation, resilience, and supportive connection.

Telehealth

Secure video sessions for clients located in Oregon.

Approach

  • Parts-work and self-leadership (IFS)
  • Safety and stabilization (TIST, somatic)
  • Values, acceptance, and committed action (ACT)
  • Humanistic–existential perspective on meaning and choice
  • Mindfulness and motivational interviewing for change
