## RESEARCH PROFILE

Interdisciplinary empirical researcher who turns difficult human questions
into defensible constructs, governed datasets, repeatable evaluation
protocols, and auditable results. Combines psychology and law with extensive
experience in regulated health technology, software quality, privacy,
security, and institutional risk. Current research examines how constrained
sociotechnical systems shape human agency, judgment, and behavior.
